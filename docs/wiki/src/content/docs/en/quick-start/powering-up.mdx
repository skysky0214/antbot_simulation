## Powering On

<Steps>

1. Press the **power button** on the lower left side of AntBot **until you hear a buzzer sound**. (Approximately 1-2 seconds)

2. The LEDs will turn on and **booting will begin**.

</Steps>

---

## Powering Off

<Steps>

1. Press the **power button** on the lower left side of AntBot **until you hear a buzzer sound**. (Approximately 1-2 seconds)

2. The LEDs will turn off and **the robot will power down**.

</Steps>
